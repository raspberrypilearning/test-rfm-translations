<h2 class="c-project-heading--task">Upload a backdrop</h2>

Upload an image backdrop so your platformer can use a world you already have.

### Starting here?

You can start here with an empty project. If you have not added the `start game` message yet, do step 1 first or use `when green flag clicked` for now.

### Choose this route if...

You already have an image on your device and want to turn it into a fast platformer background.

### Use your own backdrop file

Choose **Upload Backdrop** and pick an image from your device. A simple image with clear colours works best. Use the name Scratch gives your backdrop, or rename it first and use that name.

Add this code to the Stage:

```blocks3
when I receive [start game v]
switch backdrop to [your backdrop name v]
```


<h2 class="c-project-heading--task">Test</h2>

Click the green flag and check that your uploaded backdrop shows on the Stage.
