<h2 class="c-project-heading--task">Test and debug your platformer</h2>

Play your route from the start, spot problems, and fix the parts that do not feel right yet.

### Choose this route if...

You want to check that your choices work together before you share the game or add another level.

### Run a quick test checklist

- Does the `Player` start in the right place?
- Can the player move the way you planned?
- Can the player win?
- Can the player lose or retry in the way you planned?

If something breaks, check sprite names inside `touching` blocks, check that variables such as `Score`, `Lives`, `Time`, and `has key` are made for all sprites when needed, and check that your code is on the right sprite or the Stage.
