<h2 class="c-project-heading--task">Share your game</h2>

Get your game ready to show someone else and collect useful feedback.

### Choose this route if...

You want to hear what another player notices before you change the game again.

### Show your game to another player

Ask someone to play your platformer and tell you one thing that was fun, one thing that was tricky, and one thing they would change. Write down the answers so you can decide what to improve next.


<h2 class="c-project-heading--task">Test</h2>

Share the game with another person and make sure you collect at least one clear idea for the next improvement.
