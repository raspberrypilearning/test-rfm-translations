# Pathway Map

This file records the real branching structure for **Branching Pathways Platformer**.

## Route groups

- **Start**: set up the shared `start game` message.
- **Choose your world**: pick, draw, upload, or reuse a backdrop.
- **Choose your player**: pick, draw, upload, or reuse a player sprite.
- **Build what your player moves on**: make floors, platforms, or boundaries.
- **Choose movement**: classic jumping, simple jumps, ladder climbing, or mouse-following.
- **Shape your level**: keep it on one screen, scroll it, or add moving platforms.
- **Add a goal**: collect things, find a key, or reach an exit.
- **Decide how to win**: connect the goal to a clear win condition.
- **Add danger**: enemies, timers, spikes, or falls.
- **Decide what happens when it goes wrong**: instant game over, lives, timer loss, or respawn.
- **Add polish**: animation, sounds, win screens, game-over screens, and visual feedback.
- **Test, share, and extend**: debug the route, share it, then start another level.

## Useful loops

- After any goal route, loop back to another goal route or go to a win route.
- After any danger route, loop back to another danger route or go to a lose route.
- After any polish route, go back to testing or add more polish.
- [Start another level](https://projects.raspberrypi.org/en/projects/branching-pathways-platformer/42) loops back to world, floor, goal, danger, and win routes.
